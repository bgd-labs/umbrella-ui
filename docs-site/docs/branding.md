# Branding

![Umbrella Full_Logo](/img/umbrella-full-logo.jpg)

Official visual identity guidelines for Aave Umbrella, including logos, colors, and design elements for consistent brand representation across all platforms and communications.

## Logotype

The Aave Umbrella logo combines the Aave brand identity with an umbrella symbol, representing protection for the Aave ecosystem and its users.

![Umbrella Logo](/img/umbrella-logo-white.jpg)

## Colors

Umbrella uses yellow as its primary brand color, maintaining consistency with other Aave staking products like stkAAVE and stkGHO. Purple accents from the core Aave brand are incorporated for cohesion.

![Umbrella Colors](/img/umbrella-colors.jpg)

## Staked Tokens' Asset Frame

Staked token visual frames are designed based on the zenith view of an umbrella, creating a distinctive visual identity for Umbrella assets.

![Umbrella Frame Concept](/img/umbrella-frame-concept.jpg)

### Frame Color Guidelines

- **Yellow frame:** Used for non-Aave assets (e.g., GHO)
- **Yellow and purple frame:** Used for staked Aave aTokens, representing both Umbrella and Aave brands

![Umbrella Asset Frames](/img/umbrella-frames.jpg)
